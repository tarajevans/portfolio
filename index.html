About Me

I was born and raised just outside Peterborough, Ontario; cottage country. I grew up on Pigeon Lake and have gone back to my roots, living once again on Pigeon Lake and I love it! I am currently working full-time as a letter carrier with Canada Post Corporation while also enrolled in Carleton University's Full Stack Coding Bootcamp. My goal is to do a career change into the tech industry and obtain full-time remote employment, while fullfilling some side projects that have been on my to do list for quite some time. I learned to create webpages in the early 2000's while everything was written by hand on notepad! Over the years I have dabbled in graphic design as well as networking and hardware components. Computers just seems to be where I gravitate to!
